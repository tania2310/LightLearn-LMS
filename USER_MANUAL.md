# LightLearn LMS — User Manual

This manual describes how to use LightLearn LMS from the perspective of different user roles: Student, Mentor, and Administrator.

---

## 1. Student User Guide

### Account Setup & Access
1. **Registration**: Click "Register" on the login screen. Choose role "Student" and fill in your username, email, phone, and password.
2. **Email Verification**: Check your email inbox for the verification link to activate your account.
3. **Login**: Input your credentials on the login screen to access the Student Dashboard.

### Purchasing & Enrollment
1. **Browse Courses**: Select "Browse Courses" on the dashboard.
2. **Request Enrollment**: For paid courses, click "Buy Course" which redirects to the Checkout page. Click "Continue with PayPal" to complete transaction payments.
3. **Free Course**: For free courses, click "Request Enrollment" to register your interest (requires admin approval).

### Learning Flow
1. **Dashboard Overview**: Access approved courses, lessons progress bars, and certificate counts.
2. **Lesson details**: Click "Continue Learning" to open incomplete lesson contents (text chapters / media player assets).
3. **Quizzes**: Take quiz questions at the end of lessons to check understanding.
4. **Certificate**: Once all lessons in the course modules are completed, download your automatic certificate from the course details screen.
5. **Classroom discussion**: Access the course Discussion room to chat with your peer students and mentors.
6. **Refund request**: If you purchased a course, you can request a refund inside your dashboard. Access is revoked once approved by the admin.

---

## 2. Mentor User Guide

### Dashboard & Creation
1. **Mentor Sign-up**: Register as a Mentor (requires admin activation before login access is allowed).
2. **Create Course**: Click "Create Course". Set title, price, duration, category, and target language. All created courses start in the "Pending" status for admin review.
3. **Modules & Lessons**: Click on your course, click "Add Module" or "Create Lesson" to populate lessons, upload files, and create quiz questions.

### Moderation
1. **Respond to Q&A**: View student questions on specific lessons and respond.
2. **Moderate Chat**: Access chat rooms, delete student messages, or toggle "Lock Room" to prevent student message inputs.
3. **View Dashboard stats**: Track course counts, total lessons, student numbers, and ratings.

---

## 3. Administrator User Guide

### Access & Controls
1. **Admin Panel**: Login to `/admin` dashboard.
2. **Approve Mentors**: Review and approve mentor registrations.
3. **Approve Courses**: Review pending courses created by mentors to publish them to the active catalog.
4. **Review Moderation**: Delete invalid or toxic course reviews.
5. **Process Refunds**: Review student refund requests. Clicking "Approve" automatically refunds the payment via PayPal and revokes enrollment access.
6. **Download Reports**: Generate PDF reports for users, courses, certificates, and enrollments.
